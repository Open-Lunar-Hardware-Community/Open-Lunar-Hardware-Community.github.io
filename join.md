## Get Involved

Open Lunar is a community-driven project. We need engineers, physicists, chemists, and designers to help solve the hard problems of lunar settlement.

### How to Contribute

1.  **Code & Simulation:** Help us refine our Python models for orbital trajectories and launch physics.
2.  **CAD Design:** Contributing open-source CAD files for sled mechanisms and tank structures.
3.  **Documentation:** helping organize the wiki and research papers.
